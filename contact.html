Before You Reach Out

What happens after I submit the form?

Vaishnavi personally reviews every inquiry. You'll hear back within 1–2 business days with a short response and, if appropriate, a suggested time for a 30-minute discovery call to explore fit.

Is there a minimum engagement size?

There's no formal minimum — engagements are scoped based on the challenge and context. We work with organisations from early-stage startups to established companies of 300+ people. If the work is right, we'll find a scope that fits.

Do you work with international organisations?

Yes. While based in Mumbai, we work remotely with organisations globally and can travel for key workshops and engagements. Most of our work is hybrid — some in-person, some remote.

What if I'm not sure what I need?

That's the most common situation. The discovery call is specifically designed for this — describing what's happening in your organisation (even vaguely) is usually enough to identify where to start. You don't need a brief to get in touch.
