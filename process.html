01
Discovery & Diagnostic

Understand the people challenges before prescribing solutions

Whether we're looking at org structure, talent depth, or culture health — we start by listening. Through structured interviews and diagnostic frameworks, we build an honest picture of where your people strategy stands today and where it needs to go.

Typical Duration2–4 weeks
Key StakeholdersFounders, senior leadership, key functional heads
DeliverableDiagnostic report with prioritised findings
1

Leadership Conversations

Structured interviews with founders and senior leaders to understand growth ambitions, people constraints, and where the organisation is feeling the strain.

2

People & Org Review

A review of your current org design, role structures, talent data, and any existing people frameworks — to identify what's working and what's creating friction.

3

Diagnostic Report

A clear picture of your highest-priority people challenges — whether that's structural clarity, pipeline gaps, or culture drift — and a recommended starting point.

02
Strategy Design

Design the right solution for your organisation's stage and context

Based on diagnostic findings, we co-design the specific frameworks your organisation needs — whether that's a new org structure, a talent review process, or a values activation plan. No templates. Everything is built for where you actually are.

Typical Duration3–6 weeks, including workshops
Key StakeholdersFounders, CEO, CHRO or Head of People
DeliverablePeople strategy roadmap with 90-day priorities
1

Prioritisation Workshop

A facilitated session to align leadership on the highest-impact people priorities — org clarity, pipeline strength, or culture coherence — and build commitment to acting on them.

2

Framework Design

Development of the specific tools needed — job architecture, competency frameworks, succession maps, values and behaviour anchors, or performance systems.

3

Roadmap & Milestones

A phased plan with clear owners, timelines, and success metrics — so progress is visible and accountability is shared across leadership.

03
Implementation Support

Build it with you — not just hand it over

Frameworks only create value when they're embedded. We work alongside your people team and managers to bring new structures, processes, and practices to life — ensuring they stick well beyond the engagement.

Typical Duration6–16 weeks depending on scope
Key StakeholdersPeople team, managers, functional leads
DeliverableLive systems, trained teams, embedded practices
1

Leader & Manager Enablement

Workshops and facilitated sessions to help leaders and managers understand, adopt, and apply new structures — from performance conversations to talent calibrations.

2

Live Activation

Hands-on support as frameworks go live — facilitating first talent reviews, running culture workshops, or supporting succession planning conversations in real time.

3

Documentation & Handover

Every framework, process, and toolkit is fully documented so your team can run them confidently and independently going forward.

04
Review & Iteration

People strategy evolves — so does our work together

Organisations grow, teams change, and priorities shift. The review phase ensures your people foundations keep pace — assessing what's working, what needs adjusting, and what the next chapter of growth requires.

CadenceQuarterly check-ins, annual strategy reviews
Success MetricMeasurable outcomes vs. original baseline
DeliverableProgress report and next-phase recommendations
1

Outcome Review

Track agreed people metrics — retention, leadership bench strength, engagement, role clarity scores — against the baseline set during the diagnostic phase.

2

Leadership Alignment Sessions

Quarterly conversations to surface emerging talent challenges, stress-test existing frameworks, and keep the people agenda connected to where the business is heading.

3

Next Phase Planning

Recommendations on what to deepen, refine, or add — whether that's expanding into a new focus area or evolving existing systems as the organisation scales.

Common Questions

How long does a typical engagement take?

Most engagements run between 8–20 weeks. A focused piece of work — like designing a performance framework or running a talent review — typically sits at the shorter end. More complex org design or culture work takes longer. We scope clearly before anything starts.

Do you work with early-stage startups or only established businesses?

Both. We work with founder-led businesses building people foundations for the first time, scaling companies navigating rapid growth, and more established organisations modernising how they manage talent. The approach is always calibrated to where you are.

What does working with you look like day-to-day?

A mix of working sessions, workshops with your leadership team, and asynchronous collaboration on frameworks and documents. We stay close during implementation — facilitating talent reviews, sitting in on key conversations, and being available when real decisions are being made.

Can we start with just one focus area?

Absolutely. Most clients start with their most pressing challenge — whether that's org structure, succession, or culture — and expand from there. A short discovery call is usually enough to identify where to begin.

What if we already have an internal HR team?

We work alongside internal HR and People teams — bringing outside perspective, specialist frameworks, and additional capacity for the work that falls outside day-to-day operations. Some of our strongest partnerships have been with internal HR leads who needed a strategic thought partner.

Ready to get started?

A 30-minute discovery call is the fastest way to understand whether and how we can help.
