Leadership Pipeline

Assessing CXO Readiness for a Leading Financial Services Firm

IndustryFinancial Services
Focus AreaLeadership Pipeline
StakeholderBoard & Senior Leadership

Context

A leading financial services firm was preparing for its next phase of growth and needed clarity on the leadership capabilities required for future CXO roles. The organisation sought a structured approach to identify and develop leaders who could take on strategic responsibilities at the highest level.

Approach

Designed future-focused success profiles for business-critical leadership roles aligned with the firm's strategic priorities. Built and conducted a bespoke Assessment Centre to evaluate internal leaders against these profiles and assess their readiness for CXO positions.

Impact on Business

Provided the board with clear, evidence-based insights on the leadership pipeline and CXO readiness across the organisation. The findings informed succession planning and leadership development priorities, strengthening the firm's ability to build a future-ready executive team.

Key Outcomes

Evidence-based insights delivered to the board, informing succession planning

Bespoke success profiles built for each CXO role

Individual development plans delivered for each leader assessed
