The Framework

The Built To Scale 3P Framework

Three lenses that reveal the true foundations — or fractures — of your organisation's readiness to grow.

P
01  ·  People
Do we have the right people to grow?

Examines talent structure, leadership depth, and succession readiness.

  • Leadership pipeline clarity
  • High-potential identification
  • Role & responsibility documentation
  • Performance management systems
P
02  ·  Process
Do we have simple systems that support growth?

Uncovers bottlenecks, undocumented workflows, and decision-making gaps.

  • Documented operational workflows
  • Structured onboarding frameworks
  • Decision rights and escalation paths
  • Hiring process consistency
P
03  ·  Purpose
Are we aligned in how we work together?

Evaluates culture, values alignment, and the transmission of organisational DNA at scale.

  • Values documentation & activation
  • Culture rituals and recognition
  • Purpose-driven onboarding
  • Alignment beyond founder proximity

Our Approach

A structured six-week process

Simple, diagnostic, and entirely virtual. Four data collection methods — one clear picture of where your organisation stands.

01
Week 1

Leadership Conversations

In-depth discussions with the Founder/CEO and CHRO/HR Head to understand the business context and growth ambition.

02
Weeks 2–3

Artefact Review

Review of existing systems, frameworks, and documentation to understand what structures are in place — and what is missing.

03
Week 3–4

Self-Assessment & Employee Pulse

A structured leadership self-assessment on organisational readiness, plus a light-touch employee pulse on how systems are experienced on the ground.

04
Week 6

Walkthrough & Roadmap

A 60-minute walkthrough discussion presenting findings, insights, and a clear 0–12 month growth roadmap.

What You Get

Clear insights.
Practical direction.

At the end of six weeks, you don't get a deck full of observations. You get a clear picture, focused priorities, and a conversation to make it all actionable.

  • Simple Snapshot

    Honest insights on strengths to protect and challenges to address — in plain language.

  • 3–5 Focused Actions

    A prioritised roadmap spanning 0–3, 3–6, and 6–12 months. No overwhelming to-do lists.

Growth Roadmap Preview

Your 12-Month Path Forward

Findings are structured into a practical growth roadmap across three horizons.
