Strategic Hiring

Build hiring systems that scale with you

Most organisations hire reactively. We help you build a deliberate, repeatable hiring system that attracts candidates aligned with both the role and the company's long-term direction.

From job architecture to interview frameworks to sourcing strategy — we design every component so your hiring decisions are consistent, fast, and bias-resistant.

What's included

  • Role architecture and job family design
  • Structured interview frameworks and scorecards
  • Talent sourcing strategy by function
  • Employer brand alignment workshop
  • Onboarding experience design

02
Organisational Systems

Create the structures that let people do their best work

Unclear roles, misaligned incentives, and absent performance frameworks are among the most common — and most costly — people problems in growing organisations.

We help you design the operating backbone: role clarity, spans of control, performance frameworks, and career pathways that make your organisation feel coherent from the inside out.

What's included

  • Org design diagnostics and mapping
  • Role clarity and accountability matrices (RACI)
  • Performance framework design
  • Career pathway and levelling architecture
  • Compensation philosophy alignment
Practice Area

Organisational Systems

Leadership Pipeline

Identify and develop your next generation of leaders

Leadership transitions — whether planned or unexpected — are among the highest-stakes moments in any organisation. We help you prepare long before the moment arrives.

Through structured assessment, development planning, and facilitated conversations, we build leadership pipelines that are realistic, actionable, and tied to business strategy.

What's included

  • Leadership potential assessment framework
  • High-potential identification and mapping
  • Individual development plan design
  • Succession planning facilitation
  • Family business leadership transition support

Workforce Alignment

Close the gap between who you have and who you need

As businesses evolve, the workforce they built for yesterday can become a constraint on tomorrow. Workforce alignment work surfaces those gaps early — before they become critical.

We run talent audits, skills mapping exercises, and scenario planning sessions to help leadership teams make proactive decisions about their people portfolio.

What's included

  • Current-state workforce diagnostic
  • Skills gap analysis by function
  • Team design and span optimisation
  • Strategic workforce planning roadmap
  • Build vs. buy vs. develop decision framework
Practice Area

Workforce Alignment

05
Culture & Values

Codify your culture before it codifies itself

Culture is already operating in your organisation — the question is whether it's intentional. When left unmanaged, culture defaults to the habits and assumptions of whoever has been there longest.

We help you articulate what you genuinely stand for, translate values into behaviours, and build the processes (hiring, performance, recognition) that make culture real and durable.

What's included

  • Culture diagnostics and listening sessions
  • Values definition and behaviour anchoring workshops
  • Culture integration into hiring and onboarding
  • Recognition and reinforcement system design
  • Leadership alignment on culture commitments
Practice Area

Culture & Values
