Patterns We See

Insight

Most talent problems are diagnosed too late

By the time an organisation notices a people problem — attrition, underperformance, hiring difficulty — it has usually been building for 12–18 months. Early diagnosis is almost always cheaper than late intervention.

Insight

Culture doesn't drift — it defaults

When values aren't made explicit, culture doesn't disappear. It defaults to the norms, habits, and assumptions of whoever has been there the longest. Intentional culture design is the only way to make it yours.

Insight

Founders underestimate the cost of role ambiguity

Unclear roles aren't just an HR problem — they're a performance drag that shows up in missed deadlines, duplicated work, and frustrated people. Clarity at the role level is one of the highest-leverage investments a growing organisation can make.
