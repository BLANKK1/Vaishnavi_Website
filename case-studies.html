Fast Growing Media Company

Designing a Scalable Job Architecture for a Fast Growing Media Company

Organisational Systems

Featured Case Study

From inconsistent role levels to a structured, future-focused framework

A fast-growing Media Company was experiencing inconsistencies in role levels, compensation decisions, and career progression. As the organisation scaled, leadership needed a structured job banding and compensation framework that could support growth while maintaining fairness and market competitiveness. We designed a future-focused job banding architecture and conducted benchmarking for critical roles to ensure alignment with external market data while maintaining internal equity.

✓
Consistent, data-driven decisions on hiring, promotions & compensation enabled
✓
Transparent career progression & scalable talent framework established
